Step 2:

Freshmen REQUIREMENTS:

- Original copy of Form 138(Fourth-year high school report card or its equivalent)

- A copy of your birth certificate issued by the National Statistics Office

- Original copy of Certificate of Good Moral Character from your class adviser, guidance councelor or principal

- Three (3) 2X2" ID photos


Transferees REQUIREMENTS:

- Original or certified true copy of Transcript of Records (TOR)

- A copy of your birth certificate Issued by the National Statistics Office

- Original copy of Certificate of Transfer Eligibility ir Certificate of Transfer Credential

- Original copy of Certificate of Good Moral Character from your class adviser, guidance councelor or school principal

- Three (3) 2X2" ID photos

- Enrollment Application Form

- Original Test Results Report signed and stamped "For Enrollment"

- Original copy of the Official Receipt of Reservation (if applicable)
